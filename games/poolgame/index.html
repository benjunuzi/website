Billiards Academy

To win, pocket any object ball to claim your group: Solids (1-7) or Stripes (9-15). Once you clear all balls in your group, pocket the black 8-Ball cleanly to claim victory!
A foul occurs if you:
  • Sink the white cue ball (Scratch).
  • Strike your opponent's ball first.
  • Fail to strike any ball with your shot.
Fouls give the turn to your opponent!
Watch the projection guide! It points exactly where the target ball will go. For straight shots, aim directly through the center. For angled cut shots, line up the guide indicator on the outer side edge of the target ball.
